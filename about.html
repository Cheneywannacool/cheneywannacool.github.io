Hello! I am Jingzhi

I am an electrical engineer student turned product designer, passionate about creating products facilitate daily lives and empower people. Combining technology, business and design, I really enjoy presenting insights to solve complicated problems.

My bachelor study is about communication principles and electronic circuits. These 4-year intensive hardware-based curriculum offers me unique ways of thinking when solving user experience and interaction problems. During my undergraduate, I have designed products to conduct recover training for the elderly with dementia, and get involved in real-time movement inspection project, etc. Those valuable experiences also instruct me to develop design thinking and exploring ‘facts’ underneath facts.

I will pursue my master degree in Technology Innovation at University of Washington starting from September, 2021. My interests lie on AR/VR design and wearable devices. And currently I would like to discover data visualization deeply.
