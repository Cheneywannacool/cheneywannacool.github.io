Nice to meet you!

I am Cheney Xu, a M.S.HCI student @UW-MSTI


I am an electrical engineer turned product designer, passionate about creating products facilitate daily lives and empower people. Combining technology, business and design, I really enjoy presenting insights to solve complicated problems.
My bachelor's degree is about communication principles and electronic circuits. This 4-year intensive hardware-based curriculum offers me unique ways of thinking when solving user experience and interaction problems. During my undergraduate, I designed products to conduct recovery training for the elderly with dementia, and get involved in real-time movement inspection projects, etc. Those valuable experiences also instruct me to develop design thinking and exploring ‘facts’ underneath facts.
I will pursue my master degree in Technology Innovation at University of Washington starting from September,2021. My interests lie on AR/VR design and wearable devices. And currently I would like to discover data visualization deeply.
